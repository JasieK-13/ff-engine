How we progress & adjust
  • Strength — EASY (clean, ~3+ in the tank): +2.5kg upper / +5kg lower next week.
  • Strength — OK (clean but ~1–2 left): repeat the weight, aim +1 rep.
  • Strength — HARD (grindy / form slipped / missed reps): hold or drop 5–10% and rebuild.
  • Pull-ups: add reps until 4×10, then add load (+2.5kg).
  • Easy run: add ~3–5 min/week up to ~40–45 min. It MUST stay conversational.
  • Intervals: add 1×400m every 1–2 weeks (up to ~8–10), or nudge pace. Quality > quantity.
  • Bleep: run a real full test ~every 2 weeks to benchmark. Pacing & tight turns first — that’s where the free levels are.
  • Golden rule: progress one variable at a time, stay just shy of failure. Bad sleep/soreness → repeat the week. That’s not failure, that’s how you stay injury-free and improve faster.
